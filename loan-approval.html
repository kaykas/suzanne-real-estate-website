Mortgage Payment Calculator

The ability to check out a range of options is important because it helps you answer questions like: How much can I afford to spend on my new home?

You can enter three sets of figures into this calculator: a range of mortgage interest rates, the loan amounts you're considering, and your desired loan duration in years. The calculator then produces a table showing your monthly payment amounts across different scenarios.

Understanding that a longer or shorter loan period can markedly alter the amount of your interest payments helps you balance the components of your mortgage to fit your financial situation.
